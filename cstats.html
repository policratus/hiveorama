Statistical summary

Every data has a shape, you know it. Statistics helps us to "see" these shape, and you know it. Thus, you can see this shape using statistical tools. We bring the Statistical summary app, which calculates many statistics over your data and plot this shape on a Box-plot graph. If you want to test a sample, just select it some steps below. For this app, the sample is a stock historical data from index CNX Nifty, ranging from 2006 to 2012 (Source: NSE India - http://www.nseindia.com/).

CSV restrictions

The data must follow the format: must have no header, the first column must be a label or class (only text accepted), remaining columns must be integers or decimals and delimiter must be comma (,).
